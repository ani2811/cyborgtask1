Student Activity Centre (SAC)

Along with academic excellence, NIT Rourkela promotes extra-curricular activities to ensure all-round development
of personality. Student Activity Centre (SAC) is the central hub for student activities, student organizations,
programming, events, sports, recreation and fun at the institute campus. The Student Activity Centre
is organized into four primary societies and a host of satellite clubs. Besides, every society
has satellite clubs that have their management under the overall supervision of SAC.
The facility is devoted to student recreation and socialization to serve students,
faculty, staff, alumni, and guests.

Technical Society

Technical Society is one of the most active societies in Student Activity Centre, NIT Rourkela with eighteen Satellite Clubs under its auspices. The aim of this society is to promote and encourage technical innovation and entrepreneurship among students by organizing modeling workshops, Technical Seminars, Train-ing Courses etc. Technical society also organizes a national level Techno-management festival
every year that attracts participation from various organizations and institutions of our country.



Cyborg

Cyborg, the Robotics and Automation club of NITR is registered under the Student Activity Center. Founded in 2008, it continues to nurture brilliant minds to work diligently and shape themselves for a better year. Here we make our own robots, write our own code and build our own structures. We consider that technology is for everybody and everyone can find something interesting for themselves here.
Cyborg aims to raise the level of robotics in the institute to an international standard. The club aims to be a platform for creativity and innovation. Cyborg also participates in national and international events and conducts many events to promote the technical ability of students and enhance their performance. Our orientation is the way through which we let the freshers know about how things work here and then guide then guide them so that they can pave their way further.
